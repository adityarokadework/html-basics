Explanation of Margin, Padding, and Border

Border: The border is the edge that surrounds the content of an element. It can have different styles (solid, dashed, dotted) and thickness.

Padding: The padding is the space inside the border of an element. It creates space between the content and the border.

Margin: The margin is the space outside the border of an element. It is used to create spacing between elements.
